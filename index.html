Our Services

Aerial data acquisition and processing built around how the data gets used — by engineers, contractors, and consultants who need accuracy they can defend.

LiDAR Mapping

Point cloud generation for corridor mapping, vegetation-penetrating bare-earth models, and geohazard documentation. LAS/LAZ ready for engineering workflows.

Aerial Photogrammetry

Survey-grade orthomosaics, 3D models, and topographic mapping for construction and engineering — repeat-flight progress reporting included.

Volume Calculations

Stockpile measurements, cut/fill analysis, and earthworks monitoring with month-over-month volume comparison reports.

Oil & Gas Mapping

Lease-site documentation, ROW inspection, reclamation imagery, and tailings/berm monitoring for Alberta energy operators.
